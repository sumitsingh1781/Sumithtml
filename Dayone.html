Our services

BlackRock provides a comprehensive range of financial services globally, primarily focused on investment management, risk management, and financial technology. The company serves institutional investors, intermediaries, and individual clients across the world:

  • Investment Management: BlackRock manages equity, fixed income, balanced, real estate, and alternative investment portfolios for clients including pension funds, sovereign wealth funds, insurance companies, governments, and retail investors. It offers mutual funds, exchange-traded funds (ETFs) such as the iShares series, and alternative investment vehicles like hedge funds and structured products.
  • Technology and Data Solutions: Through its Aladdin platform, BlackRock delivers advanced risk analytics, portfolio management, trading, and compliance tools to financial institutions. This technology infrastructure supports asset managers, banks, and pension funds in managing risk and optimizing investment decisions.
  • Advisory and Risk Management Services: BlackRock’s Financial Markets Advisory (FMA) unit provides strategic guidance to financial institutions, regulators, and government entities on capital markets, regulatory challenges, balance sheet management, and financial modeling.
  • Securities Lending and Outsourced CIO Services: The firm offers securities lending to generate additional returns and outsourced chief investment officer (OCIO) services, helping institutions manage their investment portfolios with a dedicated team.
  • Wealth and Retirement Solutions: BlackRock supports individuals through retirement planning, college savings, and wealth management solutions, aiming to help clients build long-term financial security.
